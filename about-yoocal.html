Our mission

Stop checking seven different sites.

We automatically gather events from every local source — newspapers, venue websites, chamber listings, and more — and put them all in one clean, free calendar. No accounts. No paywalls. Just what's happening near you.

🔍

We do the searching

Our scrapers check dozens of local sources every day — event boards, local newspapers, venue sites, and more — so you don't have to.

✨

We clean it up

Duplicates removed, categories applied, distances calculated. Every event is organized so you can find what you're looking for in seconds.

🆓

Always free

Free for everyone, forever. We're supported by local businesses who want their events seen — not by ads or subscription fees.
